About Me

My name is Kent Okazaki. I am 21 years old and I was born and raised in San Diego. Technology has always been an interest of mine. I got myself into programming in 2011. It started off with "rooting" my first android device. In the beginning of 2012, I joined a video game modding community. The main game titles that I was involved modding with were "ArmA 2" and "ArmA 3". This is where I taught myself C++ and MySQL. For about 4 years, during high school, I continued to develop addons and content for the two games. After graduating high school, I went on to learn Java at a community college. As of 2019, I am currently taking a full stack web development bootcamp at UCSD by TrilogyEd.
